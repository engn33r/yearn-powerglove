The Analyst's Ledger

Calm, sharp, editorial DeFi analysis.

Yearn Powerglove is a dense product surface for power DeFi analysts and prosumers. It should feel composed under pressure: white analytical surfaces, precise rows, rare blue emphasis, and enough visual rhythm to keep serious data from becoming sludge.

Register

Product UI. Design serves repeated analysis, comparison, filtering, and vault-level investigation.

Personality

Calm, sharp, editorial. Intelligent without theatrics, dense without clutter.

Reject

Boring SaaS dashboards, overstuffed AI panels, gamified crypto noise.

05 Guardrails

What makes it Powerglove.

The guide is most useful when it says no. These are the rules that keep future UI from sliding into generic crypto dashboard soup.

Do
  • Use borders, row height, and alignment before adding containers.
  • Keep tables and event streams dense, but edited.
  • Make focus states visible and chart states readable without color alone.
  • Use exact, terse copy for errors, empty states, and labels.
Don't
  • Do not create boring SaaS dashboard tropes.
  • Do not create generic overstuffed AI dashboard patterns.
  • Do not create gamified slop or neon crypto terminal clichés.
  • Do not use gradient text, glassmorphism, hero metrics, or identical card grids.
